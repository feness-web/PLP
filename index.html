Recipe: Chickpea & Vegetable Stew

August 22, 2025

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups cooked chickpeas
  • 1 can (400 g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups low‑sodium broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Warm oil in a pot; sauté onion until translucent.
  2. Add carrots, pepper, and garlic; cook 3–4 minutes.
  3. Stir in chickpeas, tomatoes, broth, and cumin.
  4. Simmer 15–20 minutes; season with salt and pepper.

Nutrition & Allergen Notes

This recipe is vegetarian and naturally dairy‑free. Check labels if cooking for people with gluten or nut allergies.

Quick Kitchen Tips

  • Label and date opened items to reduce waste.
  • Keep a weekly inventory of utensils and cookware.
  • Use clean water for cooking; replace filter batteries as scheduled.
